Your 5-month roadmap
Study streak
— days in a row
Month 1 — Foundation
Master the 12 animation principles. Apply each one daily in After Effects. Build one looping animation per week minimum. Follow The Illusion of Life principles.
Month 2 — Type in motion
Kinetic typography is the fastest portfolio win. Study Alex Trochut, Buck, Ordinary Folk. Recreate 1 industry piece per week. Master expressions for text animation.
Month 3 — Intro to 3D
Start Cinema 4D. One MoGraph technique a week — cloners, effectors, dynamics. Integrate 3D into your AE workflow. Study Maxon tutorials and Greyscale Gorilla.
Month 4 — Portfolio reel
Build 3–5 strong pieces. Write case studies for each on Behance. Tailor your reel to studios you want to work with. Quality over quantity — 60 seconds max.
Month 5 — Land the job
Cold email 5 studios per week with personalised notes. Apply to School of Motion job board. Post WIP weekly on Instagram/LinkedIn. Network in MoGraph Slack communities.
