Welcome to MG Planner
Let's set up your personal schedule once — then every day adapts to it automatically.
😴
Sleep schedule
Your whole day is built around when you wake up and when you sleep. The planner will never schedule anything outside this window.
💪
Gym times
Set both your typical morning and evening gym times. Every day you'll pick which one applies — the planner adjusts everything around it.

e.g. if you get there at 6:30 am, set 6:30. The planner adds 90 min for the session + 30 min recovery.

e.g. 6:00 pm. The planner will protect your eating window and schedule work before this.

💼
Work defaults
These are your typical hours — you'll fine-tune them each day.
🔔
Notifications
The planner fires a notification at the start of each block — gym, work, MG study, eating window. Set your morning reminder time.
☁️
Sync across devices
Paste your Firebase keys to sync between phone and PC. You can skip this and add it later in Settings.

Firebase Console → Project settings → Your apps → Web → Config. See SETUP.md.
